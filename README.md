## About the Game

**MECCHA CHAMELEON** is a social camouflage game built around one core loop: **prep → disguise → hunt**.

- **Hiders** pick a spot, choose a pose, sample stage colours with Spoid, and paint their white chameleon body to match the environment.
- **Seekers** sweep the map in first person and try to spot outlines, colour breaks, and movement before time expires.
- Every round is different — there are no preset skins. Your camouflage is freehand paint, so creativity and scene reading matter as much as raw art skill.

## Game Features

### Hand-Painted Camouflage
Press **F** for the palette, use **Spoid** to sample lit and shadow tones from the stage, then left-click to paint. No two rounds look the same.

### Pose System
Press **R** to break your silhouette before painting. A good pose makes your body read as part of the scene instead of an extra outline.

### Hide & Seek Modes
- **Normal** — classic 2–4 player rounds
- **Infection** — larger 6–10 player sessions
- **Double** — works well for 4–8 players

### Official Maps
Seven built-in stages including Penguin Hotel, Sugarland, Backrooms, Mansion, Indoor Country, Sewer, and Osaka — each with distinct lighting, clutter, and sightlines.

### Steam Workshop
Official modding tools and Workshop support let the community publish custom stages. Subscribe in Steam, enable in the lobby, and test privately before hosting.

### Rankings & Feedback
Post-round scoring helps you refine routes, camouflage choices, and Seeker reads over time.

## How to Hide Effectively

A repeatable Hider workflow:

1. **Spot** — pick your location fast during prep time
2. **Pose** — match object scale and break your human outline
3. **Paint** — Spoid two tones (lit + shadow), cover visible surfaces
4. **Stay still** — movement gives you away faster than imperfect paint

In prep-limited rounds, **spot → pose → paint** beats spending the whole timer on perfect art.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is MECCHA CHAMELEON?**  
A Steam multiplayer hide-and-seek party game. Hiders paint to blend in; Seekers hunt before the timer ends.

**Are “mecha chameleon”, “mechha chameleon”, or “meccha chameleon game” the same title?**  
Yes — they are common search variants. Use the exact name **MECCHA CHAMELEON** on Steam.

**What is the Paint Hide and Seek Simulator?**  
This site’s browser practice tool. It helps you test colour matching and silhouette choices; it is not a replacement for the Steam game.

**What platforms is it on?**  
PC via Steam (Windows). Not on Roblox, mobile, or consoles at launch.

**How many players per lobby?**  
Designed for 2–10. Normal suits 2–4; Infection suits 6–10; Double works for 4–8.

**How does the paint system work?**  
F for palette, Spoid to sample stage colours, left-click to apply, R for poses. Freehand camouflage every round.

**Can you create custom maps?**  
Yes — official modding tools and Steam Workshop are supported.

**Is this the official website?**  
No. This is an unofficial player guide. If something in a guide no longer works, check [Updates](https://meccachameleon.com/updates) for your build version.
